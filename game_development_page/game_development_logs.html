2018-10-02 : Modified the enemy AI Script

I ran into an issue with the AI as even when the target was reached the target continued to ‘shimmey’ and force its way closer then I wanted the enemy to approach the player. Also when two enemy’s were next to each other the further one pushes the enemy in front to reach the player. To solve this I modified Bradley’s AI script to include a circle collider to detect when the target or another AI is reached. Thus in the script utilizing onTriggerEnter2D and onTriggerExit2D to edit a Boolean to determine whether or not to move the AI.

2018-10-03 : Modified Enemy AI Script V2 (Multiple Colliders)

Edited the AI to only chase the player when it is within the vision radius (circle collider) of the enemy and stopping the movement of the AI when it is inside the Attack radius (another circle collider). This was done by adding empty game objects; one for vision and one for attack. Respectively adding a circle collider and a script to each of the children of the enemy AI. From there I used onTriggerEnter2D and onTriggerExit2D like before to determine when it enters and leaves one of the colliders. Then within the EnemyAI script I referred to the two scripts to determine when I should move the enemy.
